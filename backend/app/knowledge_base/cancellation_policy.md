# Cancellation & Refund Policy

We know plans change, which is why AERO Airlines offers flexibility when you need to cancel or change your flight.

## 24-Hour Risk-Free Cancellation
For all tickets purchased directly through AERO Airlines, you may cancel your reservation within 24 hours of booking for a full refund to your original form of payment, provided the ticket was purchased at least 7 days prior to departure.

## Standard Economy Tickets
- **Changes:** Changes are permitted up to 2 hours before departure for a $50 change fee, plus any difference in fare. If the new flight is cheaper, you will receive travel credit for the difference minus the $50 fee.
- **Cancellations:** Standard Economy tickets are non-refundable. If you cancel, you will receive an electronic Travel Credit for the value of the ticket (minus a $100 cancellation fee). Travel credit is valid for 1 year from the date of issue.
- **No Shows:** If you do not check in and fail to cancel your flight before the scheduled departure time, the entire value of your ticket is forfeited.

## Flex Economy Tickets
- **Changes:** No change fees! You only pay the difference in fare if the new flight is more expensive.
- **Cancellations:** Flex tickets are fully refundable to your original form of payment if canceled up to 2 hours before departure.

## Delayed or Canceled Flights (AERO's Fault)
If AERO Airlines cancels your flight or delays your flight by more than 3 hours, you are entitled to:
- Be rebooked on the next available AERO flight at no extra charge.
- OR receive a full refund to your original form of payment.
- If the delay requires an overnight stay, AERO will provide hotel accommodations and meal vouchers.
