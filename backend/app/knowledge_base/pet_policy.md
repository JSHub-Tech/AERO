# Pet Travel Policy

AERO Airlines welcomes small dogs and cats to travel with you in the cabin on most flights within the United States, Canada, and Puerto Rico. 

## In-Cabin Pets
- **Fee:** $125 each way per pet carrier.
- **Carrier Requirements:** The pet must remain in a ventilated carrier that fits under the seat in front of you. Maximum dimensions for hard-sided carriers are 19"L x 15.5"W x 8.5"H. Soft-sided carriers can be slightly larger if they can compress to fit.
- **Age:** Pets must be at least 8 weeks old.
- **Booking:** There is a limit of 4 pets total per flight in the economy cabin. You must add the pet to your reservation by calling customer service at least 48 hours before departure.

## Checked Pets (Cargo)
- AERO Airlines **does not** accept pets as checked baggage or cargo, with the exception of active-duty US Military traveling on official orders.
- If you are traveling with a large dog, you must use a certified third-party pet transport service.

## Service Animals
- Fully trained psychiatric and physical service dogs travel free of charge.
- Emotional Support Animals (ESAs) are no longer recognized as service animals and must travel according to the standard In-Cabin Pet rules and pay the associated fees.
- You must complete the DOT Service Animal Air Transportation Form prior to your flight.
